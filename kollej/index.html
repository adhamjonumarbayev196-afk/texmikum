The project aims to improve practical electrical skills among students of the Tortkol 2 Polytechnic Technical College. The focus is on hands-on training in basic wiring, safety procedures, circuit installation, and the use of modern electrical tools. This project will create a modern practical training workshop equipped with electrical panels, meters, and simulation tools. It will help students gain real-world experience, improve employability, and meet industry standards in technical education.
